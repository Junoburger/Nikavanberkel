Nika van Berkel is testing boundaries between architecture, landscape, object design and art. Flowing in between conceptual thinking, researching endless possibilities and finding beauty in peculiarities. Her passion is situated in generating objects and atmospheres for people. She has graduated as an architect from the Faculty of Architecture in Ljubljana, Slovenia. At the moment residing in Rotterdam, she has worked at several architectural offices throughout Europe.
